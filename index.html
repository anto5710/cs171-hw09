📋 About This Study

This study compares two menu layouts for speed and accuracy. The session takes about 5–7 minutes.

Your job: follow the path shown on screen and click the target item as quickly and accurately as possible.
Privacy: data is anonymous and tied only to your participant ID. You may stop at any time.

🎯 Task Format

Each trial shows a full path such as Edit → Copy. Click the center start button, then follow that path to the final item.
